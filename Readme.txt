Left click to shoot
Right click for weapon special (For revolver and LMG)
WASD to move
Q to roll
B to pause

How to play
kill enemies to gain exp and level up
after 100 enemies are killed, a boss will spawn
there will be an arrow that leads you to the boss
killing the boss will give you upgrades which are way stronger than the player level up traits
kill 3 bosses and survive for 1 minute for the portal to spawn
Use the gold gained to buy more upgrades in shop to survive longer and better.
